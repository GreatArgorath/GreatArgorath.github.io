Vampire Weekend Cover

Created using Photoshop and images from The Black Archive photo gallery and Unsplash
June 21st 2025

I created this cover because the official covers for these stories started becoming really formulaic, using the same background on each one, just swapping the characters around, so for my own collections and as something for the wider community I decided to create my own alternate covers.

This story had already been announced about a year prior, and I had created my own cover just based off of the title back then, but I knew I could do better now that we had more story details, so I started again, the only element that remained was the polaroids of the vampire teeth, although now swapped out for ones more fitting of the vampire in the actual story.

The Return of the Doctor Cover

Created using Photoshop, Blender and images from The Black Archive photo gallery and Unsplash
July 3rd 2025

This was the second story in the series, and had the same cover layout as the last, so when story details were released I went straight to work on creating another.

My initial concept for the piece was having the Doctor holding the planet in her hands, but I couldn't find the right images to composite together to make it work. Instead, I came up with the idea of the Doctor in the sky looking down on the planet below, and found two images of her to composite together that fit perfectly. A friend of mine, Arbrax, managed to get the original idea to work and created it herself.

One bit I did really like from the original cover was the armadillo man, so I wanted to try and incorporate that into the piece too. Using Blender, I made a very rough mix of the head of a free armadillo 3D scan I'd found and a model of a man, minus the head. It being in sillhoutte hides how janky this model really is pretty well, so it fits right in. I placed him walking towards the TARDIS (another model I had created) and the piece was complete.

Snare Cover

Created using Photoshop and images from The Black Archive photo gallery and Unsplash
June 22nd 2025

This was the first story in a new run of 9th Doctor audios with Rose, and as with the 13th Doctor releases, they all had the same basic cover layout and background, so I wanted to create my own for my collection.

I had noticed that one of the cast members had been in the show before, although playing a different character, which made depicting them much easier, as I could use his head for a composite.

Exposure
5th December 2024

This was a project for my second year of uni, I had felt a lot of my work so far on the course was quite impersonal, and wanted to create something for a cause more important to me, so created a short film with the goal of ampolifying the voices of fellow trans and nonbinary people. I interviewed various people I knew and combined them with my own experiences to create a script for the piece, I then split it into chunks and got some of them to read out those parts of the speech. I filmed myself reading the whole thing, and replaced the audio for those parts with their speech, before they combine together with my voice towards the end, fully in sync. It's intended to represent the disconnect between what people hear about trans people and our actual experiences and what we have to say, and how the more you listen the more you might be able to understand.

The music was created by my friend Ayla Brouwer, adapted from a song she had sent me not long prior that was about her experiences with gender dysphoria.

Per Aspera

Blender Short Film
30th April 2025

This was my final project for my second year of uni, and took about 2 months to make.

During my foundation year I had started learning Blender on the side, and as one of my projects in that year I created a couple minute long short film about a robot on a spaceship hurtling towards the sun. As one of my first blender projects, it wasn't great, and I knew I could do better now with how much I'd learned since. So, with the opportunity on the moving image module in my second year, I decided to try and make a new short film. Still scifi, but much more ambitious in terms of the cinematography and realism.
